The Corn Maze

Find your way through the Corn Maze

Join us for a thrilling adventure in our Corn Maze! Navigate through twists and turns, dead ends, and surprises around every corner. Suitable for all ages, our Corn Maze offers fun challenges and a great way to spend a fall day.

The Corn Maze is open from September 15 through November 1. Come during the day for a family-friendly experience, or test your nerves at night with our Haunted Corn Maze experience!

Don't forget to stop by the snack stand for hot cider, popcorn, and other fall treats. We also have a small playground area for younger children to enjoy.

Hours

  • Mondays - Fridays: 4 pm - 8 pm
  • Saturdays: 10 am - 9 pm
  • Sundays: 11 am - 6 pm

Directions

  • From Council Bluffs, proceed east on I-80
  • Take Exit 38 North to the Drake Frontage Road
  • Turn right on Highway G
  • Proceed east for 2.5 miles
  • Sunny Acres is on your left; watch for the green sign
