Welcome to Sunny Acres

Welcome to Sunny Acres, a place where family fun and adventure await you year-round. Nestled in the heart of Iowa, Sunny Acres offers a variety of activities and attractions for visitors of all ages. Whether you're navigating the twists and turns of our Corn Maze, experiencing the thrills of our Haunted Barn, or simply enjoying a peaceful day in the Petting Barn, there's something for everyone.

Our farm is more than just a place to visit; it's a place to create memories. With seasonal events and activities, there's always something new to explore at Sunny Acres. Don't forget to stop by The Farm Shop for some farm-fresh goods and unique gifts to take home.

Explore the beauty of our farm, enjoy the festive atmosphere during the holidays, and make the most of your visit with family and friends. We look forward to welcoming you to Sunny Acres!
